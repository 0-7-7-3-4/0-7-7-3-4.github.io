Travel to Diamond Bar

Authors: Bonnie Nien and Grace Makahaube

A small residential city in eastern Los Angeles County, California, USA. You'll see plenty of average citizens living a normal and interconnected life in this close knit community. Ready to learn more?!?

Popular Sites

LA County Library

  • Children Friendly
  • Plenty of activities and resources for students
  • Quiet and Neat

An ideal place to relax, study, and enjoy reading. Hosts numerous fun activities for little children to encourage their interest in learning and literature. Friendly staff, clean environment, and a variety of genres in books to keep you entertained.

Summitridge Park
  • Spacious hiking trail
  • Relaxing nature
  • Breathtaking views

A good chance to see wildlife and enjoy nature by itself. Trees offer cool shade and a wide trail provides safety and comfort while still challenging you with appropriate hills and other ups-and-downs.
